About

The Person
Behind the Work

"Passionate about visual storytelling, on-set work, and observing the small details that create strong visual impact."

I'm Abdullah El Shamy — a production professional based in Egypt with hands-on experience across advertising, music video, and commercial productions. Over the past six months I've worked on more than 30 projects spanning assistant directing, casting coordination, and BTS documentation.

My time at Kivya deepened my understanding of production workflow and team coordination in professional creative environments. I've had the opportunity to work alongside major Egyptian and international brands, and with some of the region's most recognized public figures and artists.

I'm looking to grow within a creative production environment where discipline, visual instinct, and practical knowledge are valued.
